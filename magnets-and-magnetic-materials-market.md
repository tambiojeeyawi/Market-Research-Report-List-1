<p><ul><li>Semi-hard Magnet</li><li>Soft Magnet</li><li>Permanent/Hard Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnets and magnetic materials market is categorized into three main types: semi-hard magnets, soft magnets, and permanent (hard) magnets. </p><p>Semi-hard magnets exhibit moderate magnetic properties and retain some magnetization after the external field is removed, useful in applications like loudspeakers. Soft magnets, characterized by low coercivity, can quickly magnetize and demagnetize, making them ideal for transformers and inductors. Permanent magnets, or hard magnets, maintain their magnetization without external power, commonly used in motors, generators, and magnetic storage devices. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries.</p></p>

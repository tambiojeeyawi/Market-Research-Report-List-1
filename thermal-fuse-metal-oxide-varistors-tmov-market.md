<p><p>Thermal Fuse Metal Oxide Varistors (TMOV) are advanced surge protection devices that combine the properties of thermal fuses and metal oxide varistors. Their dual-action capability provides a reliable mechanism to disconnect from circuit under excessive heat while also clamping voltage surges.</p><p>The market for TMOVs is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and reliable protection solutions in various sectors.
